# KTS | Boxing Beta Clan
KTS is the official website and management platform for the KTS Boxing Beta Clan in Roblox. Created by esdeekido, the platform serves as the central hub for clan members, showcasing the roster, achievements, events, and community while providing tools to manage the organization.

Built for the KTS community, the website represents the clan's identity and streamlines communication and member management.

## Live Website

🌐 https://ktsofficial.vercel.app/

## Purpose

KTS was developed as the official online presence of the KTS Boxing Beta Clan. It provides members and visitors with a centralized location to explore the clan, stay updated on events, view member information, and strengthen the community both in and outside of Roblox.

Whether you're a current member or someone interested in joining, KTS serves as the primary destination for everything related to the clan.
